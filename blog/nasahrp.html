KinKinetics at NASA HRP 2024

A Remarkable Experience in Advancing Space Research

February 2024 • NASA Human Research Program

The NASA Human Research Program (HRP) Conference 2024 brought together the brightest minds in space research, and KinKinetics was honored to be part of this extraordinary gathering. Presenting our poster at this prestigious event marked a pivotal milestone in our journey to support human adaptation in extreme environments.

Presenting KinKinetics at the NASA HRP Conference 2024

Walking through the halls of groundbreaking research, we engaged with leading scientists, astronauts, engineers, and decision-makers dedicated to addressing the physiological and psychological challenges of long-duration spaceflight. The atmosphere buzzed with innovation and collaboration.

One of the most rewarding aspects was diving deep into conversations about human factors in space exploration, decision-making under stress, and the integration of neuroeconomics into astronaut support systems—the very foundation of KinKinetics.

Engaging with researchers on neuroeconomic applications in space

Our poster on neuroeconomic-based decision support for long-duration missions resonated deeply with attendees, sparking insightful discussions on practical applications and future collaborations.

The feedback we received from researchers and industry experts was invaluable, helping us refine our approach to integrating physiological and behavioral insights into real-time decision-making tools for space travelers. The enthusiasm surrounding our work reinforced its potential impact on future missions.

Connecting with pioneers in human spaceflight research

Perhaps the most inspiring aspect was connecting with visionaries in human spaceflight research. From pre-award research managers to astronauts and behavioral scientists, each conversation opened doors to new ideas. We explored possibilities for funding support, research partnerships, and opportunities to elevate KinKinetics to the next stage.

As we reflect on this incredible experience, one thing is clear—KinKinetics has an exciting path ahead. The insights gained from NASA HRP 2024 will shape our next steps, helping us refine our technology and contribute meaningfully to human adaptation in space and extreme environments.

A heartfelt thank you to everyone who engaged with us, shared their expertise, and provided constructive feedback. The journey of KinKinetics is just beginning, and we are more motivated than ever to push the boundaries of what's possible.

The Future is Bright

Stay tuned as we continue to pioneer neuroeconomic solutions for human space exploration. The next chapter of KinKinetics is being written, and we're thrilled to have you along for the journey.
