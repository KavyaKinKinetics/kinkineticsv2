About KinKinetics

A Closed Loop That Keeps Decisions Stable Under Pressure

KinKinetics is a deep-tech venture building a closed-loop neuroadaptive wearable: it senses physiological stress in real time, predicts the moment decision quality is about to break down, and intervenes to restore it.

The science is the moat. We combine autonomic physiology, machine learning, and neuroeconomic models of how decisions fail under time pressure — a system that grows more accurate the more it is used.

We start where the need is sharpest and the path to real-world data is clearest — clinicians under sustained stress — then scale the same core system to high-stakes operators and, ultimately, to long-duration space missions.

The System

One Closed Loop. Three Markets.

The same sense–predict–intervene core, delivered as a research-grade stack today and hardened for higher-stakes settings as the evidence compounds.

Sense

Continuous physiological sensing — the autonomic signatures of stress and cognitive load — from a lightweight wearable.

  • Electrodermal activity
  • Heart rate & HRV
  • Continuous capture

Predict

A signal-processing and machine-learning pipeline turns raw physiology into a live cognitive state — and is being extended to flag decision instability before it happens.

  • Real-time state estimation
  • Decision-instability model
  • Personalised thresholds

Intervene

A closed-loop response tied to state: real-time visual and haptic feedback today; non-invasive vagus-nerve stimulation on the roadmap.

  • Real-time feedback
  • Non-invasive taVNS (roadmap)
  • Adapts to measured state

Healthcare — Beachhead

Clinicians and human-factors labs under sustained stress. Research-grade, with no medical-device blocker — the fastest route to real-world evidence.

  • Clinician stress
  • Research-grade stack
  • Builds the evidence base

High-Stakes Operators — Next

Emergency services, aviation and surgical training — roles where a single degraded decision is costly.

  • Resilience under load
  • Operator performance
  • Training programmes

Extreme Environments — Long Horizon

Long-duration spaceflight, isolated and confined stations, and defence — autonomous decision support where crew cognition is mission-critical.

  • Microgravity & isolation
  • Cognitive countermeasures
  • Autonomous support
